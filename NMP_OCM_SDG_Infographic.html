Strongest Coverage

Quality Education, Life on Land & Responsible Consumption

SDG 4, 15, and 12 recorded the most documented practices, with NMP offices nationwide reporting programs across nearly every assessed target — from biodiversity research to sustainable lifestyles education.

Growth Opportunity

Clean Water, Good Health & Reduced Inequalities

SDG 6, SDG 3, and SDG 10 show the fewest reported practices relative to assessed targets — these represent concrete opportunities to deepen collections-based programming, research, and partnerships.

Cross-Cutting Theme

Gender & Inclusion Runs Through All Goals

From GAD-aligned public programs and women-led livelihood workshops (SDG 1, 5) to exhibitions addressing violence and child exploitation (SDG 16), NMP offices consistently embed gender-responsive approaches.
